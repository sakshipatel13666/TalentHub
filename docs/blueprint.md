# **App Name**: TalentHub

## Core Features:

- Secure User Authentication & Role Selection: Users can sign up, log in, and choose their primary role (Talent Creator, Client, Student, Admin) to access personalized platform features.
- Comprehensive Talent Profile Management: Talent Creators can create and manage detailed profiles, including basic information, profile photo, bio, skills, experience, portfolio (images, videos, external links), services offered, and pricing.
- Advanced Talent Discovery & Search: Clients and Students can explore talents and workshops using robust search functionality, categories, and filters such as skill, price range, rating, and location, with talent cards showing key information.
- Hiring & Project Request System: Clients can send job requests to talents with project descriptions, budget, deadlines, and communicate via an integrated messaging system, tracking job status through a dedicated dashboard.
- Workshop Creation & Enrollment: Talent Creators can design and list workshops or classes with titles, descriptions, categories, pricing, duration, dates, and maximum participants. Students can browse, enroll, and view upcoming classes.
- Reviews & Ratings System: After job or workshop completion, users can leave star ratings and written reviews, which are publicly displayed on talent and workshop profiles to build trust and credibility.
- AI-Powered Profile Content Assistant: An AI tool helps Talent Creators generate compelling bios, optimize portfolio descriptions, or craft engaging workshop summaries, making their profiles more attractive and discoverable.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color Palette Scheme: Light. This choice allows vibrant accents to pop against a clean, modern canvas, fostering an engaging and visually dynamic experience suited for showcasing diverse talents and fostering learning.
- Primary Color: #564CB3 (a deep, elegant lavender-indigo). This sophisticated shade embodies innovation, trust, and creativity, setting a professional yet inspiring tone for the platform without being overtly 'techy'.
- Background Color: #F4F3F6 (a very subtle, cool grey-white). Visibly of the same hue as the primary color but heavily desaturated, it provides a clean, neutral canvas that enhances readability and allows content and vibrant elements to stand out gracefully.
- Accent Color: #4D89F2 (a vibrant medium blue). Analogous to the primary but with higher saturation and brightness, this accent color provides strong contrast and a sense of energy, ideal for interactive elements like buttons, highlights, and notifications to guide user attention.
- Headline Font: 'Space Grotesk' (proportional sans-serif). This choice lends a modern, structured, and slightly tech-inspired aesthetic to titles, reflecting the 'Hub' aspect of the platform's name.
- Body Font: 'Inter' (grotesque-style sans-serif). Paired with Space Grotesk, Inter ensures exceptional readability and a clean, neutral presentation for all descriptive content, profiles, and smaller text, maintaining a polished and professional look.
- Icons will be modern, linear, and minimalistic. They will be consistently styled to enhance navigation clarity and visual harmony across the platform, using subtle animations for user feedback.
- A responsive, mobile-first grid system will ensure optimal display and usability across all devices. Layouts will emphasize ample white space for a clean, breathable feel, allowing talent portfolios and workshop details to be the focal point without clutter.
- Subtle and purposeful animations will be employed for transitions between pages, hover states, and dynamic content loading. These animations will enhance the user experience by providing clear visual feedback and making interactions feel more fluid and engaging without being distracting.
